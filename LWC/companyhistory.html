humble beginnings


This is how the story goes: John "Jack" Long (senior, 1928-2013) left the Hatoro post office in on a chilly day in November to head home after a long days work. Meticulous as he was, he couldn't help but notice the salt had mixed with the snow and dirtied the windows of the post office. And so it began.

Soon enough, he was servicing all the windows of historic Main Street Hatboro.

the early years


Jack grew the business from his home office and enrolled his son in some hard work. Soon enough, they moved beyond the windows on Main Street Hatboro and the business began to grow. As the business grew, so did the need to higher someone who could keep track of all the files and accounting. And so the first employee was hired.

growth


In 1988, operations were under way and LWC had a modest fleet of 3 vehicles and staffed 8 men. They operated out of a warehouse in Willow Grove.

As new client relationships formed and LWC spread to serve the entire Delaware Valley, they outgrew the space and are now located in Willow Grove on Maryland Road.

today


LWC Services now has a team of nearly 100 employees. The customer base has grown widely, and many new services and niche specialties have gone underway.

We want to thank our new and existing clients for helping us grow to where we are today.
