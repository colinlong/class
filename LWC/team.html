meet the team

james long, founder and ceo

The founder and owner of LWC Services, he began his journey in 1950 when his father handed him a bucket and a squeegee. He's since served as president of the IWCA and is a long-term appointed board member. Find him in the mountains when he's not on the clock.

mark gosni, operations

A multi-faceted coordinator of all LWC Service's actions. He works the front line to ensure quality and consistency with each new project. He obtained his B.S. in Management from Philadelphia University.

ashley when, accounts

A talented multi-tasking account manager who works to make sure all is in order. Ashley obtained her B.S. in Accounting from Drexel University.

bill yerkes, management

A multi-faceted coordinator of team projects and endeavors. Bill travels from job site to job site to achieve consistency.

michelle unger, training

She works with the team of window cleaners and maintenance crew to train them on the OSHA safety certifications and works to be compliant with all ANSI standards. Michelle obtained her M.S. in Safety Science from Indiana University of Pennsylvania

connor vale, client coordinations

Connor makes sure all client needs are met through meetings and regular contact. He will work with you to set up any scheduling service type you need.
