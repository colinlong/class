overview


We hand clean both the interior and exterior windows. We include the cleaning of all your window panes, ledges, frames, screens, and sills to perfection. LWC Services only uses environmentally safe, bio-degradable solutions so it is safe for children, pets, and you.

When cleaning your windows, all window screens are removed and wiped down. More extensive screen cleaning services are available upon request. If any overspray of paint is found on the glass, it will carefully be removed with a scraper.

LWC Services focuses on detailing each window to perfection, leaving you with perfectly clean windows and a timely service that does not interrupt your entire day.

Residential window cleaning prices are determined by the window type and the number of windows there are. A price quote can easily be done over the phone, by email or a free onsite quote done by one of our team members.

We have a 100% satisfaction guarantee on our entire window cleaning job.

glass restoration + repair


LWC Services specializes in glass restoration service and hard water stain removal in addition to a “soap and water” cleaning of the windows at your home in Philadelphia and the Delaware Valley.

The most common form of glass damage occurring in residences is glass corrosion from hard water stains. Hard water stains or ‘water spots’ are formed when evaporated water, usually from sprinkler systems, leaves calcium, magnesium and other mineral deposits on your windows.

95% of all hard water stains are stage one glass corrosion and can be removed with our hard water stain removal treatment. The other 5% occur when untreated hard water stains have caused permanent etching in the glass. To prevent this from happening to your windows, hard water stain removal needs to be performed on a regular basis.

Our highly skilled technicians can restore any type of glass at your commercial or residential property for only a fraction of the cost of replacement. Not only do scratches obstruct visibility, they also create structural stresses in glass. By having LWC Services remove glass scratches, clear visibility and structural soundness are restored while saving time and money.
